About the Sanctuary of Freewheeling Relgion

Founded in 2018 by a visionary urban planner and a civil rights sheikh, the Sanctuary of Freewheeling views driving as a spiritual expression of freedom. Advocating for the belief that fines impede this sacred right, they promote driving as a crucial part of personal and communal connection. Their efforts are geared towards reshaping policies that affect drivers, especially under restrictive traffic regulations.

Official Belief System

  1. The Divine Road Doctrine

    Driving is a sacred act enabling personal enlightenment and spiritual connection. Sacred texts like "The Wheel's Path" guide the congregation, with symbols such as the steering wheel and open road held as holy icons.

  2. Ethical Driving Code

    Members adhere to ethical driving, including mindfulness and respect on the road, seeing fines as impediments to spiritual freedom. Community obligations focus on road safety and environmental stewardship.

Official Practices and Rituals

  1. Rituals of Movement

    Members undertake spiritual drives along sacred routes, reflecting on personal journeys and spiritual guidance through regular "Ceremonies of the Compass."

  2. Pilgrimages on Wheels: Members undertake spiritual drives along designated sacred routes, which are chosen for their ability to inspire spiritual contemplation and connection. These pilgrimages are central rituals in the congregation's practice.

Community Engagement and Advocacy

  • Workshops and Seminars: Educating the public on the spiritual dimensions of driving.
  • Advocacy for Spiritual Rights: Participating in legal and political forums to recognize driving as a religious practice.
  • Interfaith Cooperation: Collaborating with other spiritual groups to promote understanding of diverse spiritual expressions.

Documentation and Institutional Evidence

  • Religious Activities Log: Keeping detailed records of all religious activities.
  • Financial Transparency: Maintaining comprehensive financial records to substantiate the non-profit status.
  • Member Narratives: Compiling testimonials that reflect the spiritual impact of driving.
